    1: 2022-06-07 09:52:00+08:00 推文

    云南省教育厅原厅长罗崇敏，因在微信公众平台发文《端午：一个鼓励自杀的日子》，被指侮辱英烈，现被云南省教育厅调查。该文章称刘胡兰、刘文学和王二小等人是不好的榜样，因为他们是“忠君爱国的自杀者”。

    2: 2022-06-07 09:57:00+08:00 推文

    Luo Chongmin, former director of Education Department of Yunnan Province, now under investigation for an article he posted on Internet which authorities claimed “insulted heroes and martyrs”. He denounced the propaganda about some officially recognized martyrs in the article.

    3: 2022-06-08 10:58:00+08:00 推文

    广东省佛山市居民黄某，因在公共场合张贴传单，内容为“以谐音方式辱骂国家领导人”，于2020年1月被当地检方批捕，案件后续不详。

    4: 2022-06-08 11:02:00+08:00 推文

    Guangdong resident Huang, who sticked some poster publicly that “insulted national leaders in a homophonous way”, arrested in Jan 2020. Subsequent update of the case remain unknown.

    5: 2022-06-09 07:53:37+08:00 推文

    4月16日，当时的哈尔滨才解封不到一星期，当地居民唐某敏因在微信群中发布“要封城了”的“谣言”，被处行政拘留。就在第二天，17日深夜，哈尔滨政府宣布进入严控状态，“谣言”成真。

    6: 2022-06-09 08:00:30+08:00 推文

    On Apr 16, police in Harbin detained a local netizen surnamed Tang for spreading a “rumor” that the city would soon be locked down due to Covid, which had been then lifted for only a few days. The very next night, Harbin gov’t announced covid lockdown, making the “rumor” true.

    7: 2022-06-12 12:50:00+08:00 推文

    广东省东莞市异议人士田维权，因在Telegram、YouTube和推特“辱骂政府和国家领导”以及发表“有关政府、国家领导人、国内重事件等的虚假信息”，于2020年12月被判处有期徒刑两年，二审维持原判。



    8: 2022-06-12 12:56:00+08:00 推文

    Guangdong dissident Tian Weiquan, who “insulted the government and national leaders and posted false information about the government, national leaders and major incidents of the country” on Telegram, YouTube and Twitter, sentenced to 2 years in prison.
