   买一把锤子就会能让你变成“恐怖分子”

 <div class="entry-content">
  <p class="hl hm eq bh ho b hp hq hr hs ht hu hv hw hx hy hz" data-selectable-paragraph="">
   如果你因任何原因需要一把锤子，英国的反恐警察部队就会认为你是一个潜在的“恐怖分子”。 他们还明确要求群众斗群众：任何看到某人做出可疑事的人 — — 比如购买锤子，就立即向执法者报告。
  </p>
  <p class="hl hm eq bh ho b hp hq hr hs ht hu hv hw hx hy hz" data-selectable-paragraph="">
   根据英国反恐警察办公室发布的推文，购买锤子是可疑的并且可能是犯罪活动的指标。
  </p>
  <p data-selectable-paragraph="">
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6177 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 998px) 100vw, 998px" data-lazy-src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=998%2C1280&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?w=998&amp;ssl=1 998w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=234%2C300&amp;ssl=1 234w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=768%2C985&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=798%2C1024&amp;ssl=1 798w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="1280" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=998%2C1280&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="998"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6177" data-recalc-dims="1" height="1280" sizes="(max-width: 998px) 100vw, 998px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=998%2C1280&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?w=998&amp;ssl=1 998w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=234%2C300&amp;ssl=1 234w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=768%2C985&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/0-267.png?resize=798%2C1024&amp;ssl=1 798w" width="998"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p class="hl hm eq bh ho b hp hq hr hs ht hu hv hw hx hy hz" data-selectable-paragraph="">
   英国还有一个滑稽的活动正在进行，试图说服年轻人
   <a class="aq cc iw ix iy iz" href="https://ycyf.co.uk/">
    不要带任何刀具
   </a>
   。
  </p>
  <p class="hl hm eq bh ho b hp hq hr hs ht hu hv hw hx hy hz" data-selectable-paragraph="">
   需要一些刀具和一把锤子实际上可以有一个非常合乎逻辑且可能的解释。这个男人可能是一名厨师或者是个木工，但这个广告宣传活动的目的是他们实际上不希望你想到最可能的这种解释。他们希望你怀疑所有人，甚至害怕自己无法正常生活。完成可怕的“生活没有倒带按钮”的座右铭。
  </p>
  <p class="hl hm eq bh ho b hp hq hr hs ht hu hv hw hx hy hz" data-selectable-paragraph="">
   Reason 指出，整个视频广告实际上更加险恶，不是因为它根本不可思议，而是英国反恐警察要求你审查自己和他人的一切行为，不论是什么原因：包括把垃圾拿走这种简单的事。看视频：
  </p>
  <p data-selectable-paragraph="">
   <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" height="421" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/RX6x1HuOWeU" width="750">
   </iframe>
  </p>
  <p data-selectable-paragraph="">
   他们[反恐警察]试图鼓励人们互相监视互相举报，导致人们最终互相举报无辜的活动。这些调查很可能会颠覆无辜者的整个生活。这个广告不仅是可以模仿的，而且是令人恐惧的。你应该警惕的是那些当权者和他们制造的警察国家，而不是身边的和你一样的老百姓。
  </p>
  <p data-selectable-paragraph="">
   <span class="n in io ip iq ir is it iu iv cu">
    If
   </span>
   <a class="aq cc iw ix iy iz" href="https://www.zerohedge.com/news/2019-02-01/totalitarianism-buying-hammer-now-makes-you-terrorist-uk">
    you need a hammer for any reason, the United Kingdom’s counter-terrorism policing unit will consider you a potential terrorist
   </a>
   . They are also asking that anyone who sees someone doing something suspicious, like purchasing a hammer, to report it to the enforcers.
  </p>
