   <h1>
    波斯纳法官“无情杀戮”反同性恋婚姻主张
   </h1>
   <ul class="post-entry-meta">
    <li>
     By:
     <a href="http://www.ideobook.com/about-ideobook/" title="查看 爱德布克 的作者主页">
      爱德布克
     </a>
     . 2014-8-28.
     <a href="http://www.ideobook.com/372/post-views-count/" title="统计说明">
      4,061
     </a>
    </li>
   </ul>
  </header>
  <div class="post-entry-content">
   <p>
    Above the Law 的报道
    <a href="http://abovethelaw.com/2014/08/judge-posners-blistering-benchslaps-at-the-same-sex-marriage-arguments/">
     “Judge Posner’s Blistering Benchslaps At The Same-Sex Marriage Arguments”
    </a>
    ：
   </p>
   <blockquote>
    <p>
     As we mentioned in
     <a href="http://abovethelaw.com/2014/08/morning-docket-08-27-14/">
      Morning Docket
     </a>
     and on
     <a href="https://twitter.com/DavidLat/status/504398270131470336">
      Twitter
     </a>
     , yesterday’s Seventh Circuit arguments weren’t fun for the defenders of Wisconsin and Indiana’s same-sex marriage bans. The three judges, especially Judge Richard Posner, were tough — very tough.
    </p>
    <p>
     Chris Geidner of
     <a href="http://www.buzzfeed.com/chrisgeidner/indiana-wisconsin-lawyers-face-tough-questions-in-appellate">
      BuzzFeed
     </a>
     , a leading chronicler of marriage-equality litigation, described the proceedings as “the most lopsided arguments over marriage bans at a federal appeals court this year.” Ian Millhiser of
     <a href="http://thinkprogress.org/justice/2014/08/26/3476116/federal-appeals-court-judge-ban-on-same-sex-marriage-is-based-on-hate/">
      ThinkProgress
     </a>
     called it “a bloodbath.”
    </p>
    <p>
     That’s no exaggeration. Let’s check out the specifics….
    </p>
    <p>
     A Seventh Circuit panel consisting of Judge Richard Posner, Judge Ann Claire Williams, and Judge David F. Hamilton heard argument on Tuesday in two cases:
     <a href="http://www.lambdalegal.org/in-court/cases/baskin-v-bogan">
      <em>
       Baskin v. Bogan
      </em>
     </a>
     , a challenge to Indiana’s statutory ban on same-sex marriage, and
     <a href="http://www.freedomtomarry.org/litigation/entry/wisconsin">
      <em>
       Wolf v. Walker
      </em>
     </a>
     , a challenge to Wisconsin’s constitutional ban on same-sex marriage. You can listen to the arguments
     <a href="http://media.ca7.uscourts.gov/sound/external/rt.1.14-2386_08_26_2014.mp3">
      here (
      <em>
       Baskin
      </em>
      )
     </a>
     and
     <a href="http://media.ca7.uscourts.gov/sound/external/rt.2.14-2526_08_26_2014.mp3">
      here (
      <em>
       Wolf
      </em>
      )
     </a>
     .
    </p>
   </blockquote>
   <p>
    <span id="more-2182">
    </span>
   </p>
   <p>
    除了这篇报道，Slate 的报道 “
    <a href="http://www.slate.com/blogs/outward/2014/08/27/listen_to_judge_richard_posner_destroy_arguments_against_gay_marriage.html">
     Listen to a Conservative Judge Brutally Destroy Arguments Against Gay Marriage
    </a>
    ” 也值得一看，尤其是如果您没有时间听全部庭审录音的话。它剪辑出了十段不长的录音，是
    <a href="http://posner.ideobook.com">
     波斯纳法官
    </a>
    的一些重要的“杀戮”片段。非常精彩，且有娱乐性。必听！
   </p>
   <p>
    补记：印第安纳、威斯康辛两州的同性恋婚姻禁令被美国联邦第七巡回区上诉法院推翻，见
    <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2014/09/05/us/us-appellate-court-overturns-same-sex-marriage-bans-in-wisconsin-and-indiana.html">
     《纽约时报》报道
    </a>
    （还有
    <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2014/09/05/opinion/a-blunt-defense-of-marriage-equality.html">
     社论
    </a>
    ）。波斯纳代表法院撰写了
    <a href="http://media.ca7.uscourts.gov/cgi-bin/rssExec.pl?Submit=Display&amp;Path=Y2014/D09-04/C:14-2526:J:Posner:aut:T:fnOp:N:1412339:S:0">
     判决书
    </a>
    。
   </p>
