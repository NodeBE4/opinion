   Criptext 是一种新的加密电子邮件服务，不存储您的电子邮件

  <p>
   与其他加密电子邮件服务不同，Criptext 并不掌握用户的密钥，还是由用户自行生成并且存储在设备上。这就意味着，只有发件人和收件人双方能够知晓邮件内容
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9074 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 1100px) 100vw, 1100px" data-lazy-src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=1100%2C516&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?w=1710&amp;ssl=1 1710w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=300%2C141&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=768%2C360&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=1024%2C480&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=1100%2C516&amp;ssl=1 1100w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="516" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=1100%2C516&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="1100"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9074" data-recalc-dims="1" height="516" sizes="(max-width: 1100px) 100vw, 1100px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=1100%2C516&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?w=1710&amp;ssl=1 1710w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=300%2C141&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=768%2C360&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=1024%2C480&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0.png?resize=1100%2C516&amp;ssl=1 1100w" width="1100"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   Criptext 是一种新的加密电子邮件服务，“不会在其服务器中存储任何电子邮件”，而是“所有电子邮件都存储在您的设备上”。
   <strong>
    对用户所发送的消息内容进行端对端加密
   </strong>
   。
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   Criptext 电子邮件于 2018 年8月作为测试版服务推出，它有一个相当有趣的原始故事，如果您有兴趣，可以
   <a class="_2qJYG blog-link-hashtag-color _3sz0l" href="https://venturebeat.com/2018/08/14/criptexts-mizrachi-escapes-from-a-colombian-prison-to-launch-a-secure-email-platform/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
    <u class="sDZYg">
     在这里
    </u>
   </a>
   阅读。
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   访问您的 Criptext 帐户只能通过使用它的
   <a class="_2qJYG blog-link-hashtag-color _3sz0l" href="https://github.com/Criptext" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
    <u class="sDZYg">
     开源应用程序
    </u>
   </a>
   来完成（适用于 macOS，Linux，Android和 iOS;即将推出 Windows 版）该服务现在可以免费使用。付费高级版可能会在未来推出，但免费版将继续推出。
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9075 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 1100px) 100vw, 1100px" data-lazy-src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=1100%2C702&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?w=1688&amp;ssl=1 1688w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=300%2C192&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=768%2C490&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=1024%2C654&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=1100%2C702&amp;ssl=1 1100w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="702" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=1100%2C702&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="1100"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9075" data-recalc-dims="1" height="702" sizes="(max-width: 1100px) 100vw, 1100px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=1100%2C702&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?w=1688&amp;ssl=1 1688w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=300%2C192&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=768%2C490&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=1024%2C654&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-1.png?resize=1100%2C702&amp;ssl=1 1100w" width="1100"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   Criptext 电子邮件特点包括：
  </p>
  <ul class="_2PG9L Ryx7c">
   <li class="_1BJwx _208Ie _2Dym_ Dd5y9 _2KQhc _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
    <p class="_208Ie">
     端到端加密
    </p>
   </li>
   <li class="_1BJwx _208Ie _2Dym_ Dd5y9 _2KQhc _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
    <p class="_208Ie">
     “取消发送”电子邮件
    </p>
   </li>
   <li class="_1BJwx _208Ie _2Dym_ Dd5y9 _2KQhc _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
    <p class="_208Ie">
     显示收件人读取
    </p>
   </li>
   <li class="_1BJwx _208Ie _2Dym_ Dd5y9 _2KQhc _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
    <p class="_208Ie">
     开源应用程序
    </p>
   </li>
  </ul>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   Criptext 使用自己的唯一密钥加密每封电子邮件，因此即便密钥被泄露，您的收件箱也是安全的。此外，应用程序仅在您的设备上生成并存储加密密钥，而不是像 ProtonMail，在其安全页面上作为示例给出。
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   引起我注意的是 Criptext 声称它不会将电子邮件存储在其服务器上。根据其
   <a class="_2qJYG blog-link-hashtag-color _3sz0l" href="https://criptext.com/faq/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
    <u class="sDZYg">
     常见问题页面
    </u>
   </a>
   ，有一些例外。
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   例如，如果您已登录设备但未启动或未连接到 Internet，则电子邮件将存储在 Criptext 服务器上，直到您的设备能够接收电子邮件（已启动并连接到 Internet）。
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   作为旁注，您还应该知道您需要登录至少一台设备，否则您将丢失任何可能收到的电子邮件。由于加密，
   <strong>
    如果没有活动密钥，则无人可以访问传入的电子邮件。
   </strong>
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   在“您存储关于我的哪些信息？”的回答中提到：“我们从不存储您的电子邮件，除非它是发送到非 Criptext 电子邮件地址的，在这种情况下，它会暂时存储在我们的服务器中。”
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   此外，电子邮件附件（每封电子邮件限制为 20MB）在 Amazon Web Services 上加密存储。
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   如果您想知道，是的，您可以使用 Criptext 接收非加密电子邮件，如果您愿意，也可以发送未加密的电子邮件。使用 Criptext 发送到其他 Criptext 电子邮件地址的电子邮件始终是加密的。
  </p>
  <figure aria-describedby="caption-attachment-9076" class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_9076" style="width: 1690px">
   <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-9076 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 1100px) 100vw, 1100px" data-lazy-src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=1100%2C736&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?w=1690&amp;ssl=1 1690w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=300%2C201&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=768%2C514&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=1024%2C685&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=1100%2C736&amp;ssl=1 1100w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=272%2C182&amp;ssl=1 272w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="736" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=1100%2C736&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="1100"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-9076" data-recalc-dims="1" height="736" sizes="(max-width: 1100px) 100vw, 1100px" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=1100%2C736&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?w=1690&amp;ssl=1 1690w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=300%2C201&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=768%2C514&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=1024%2C685&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=1100%2C736&amp;ssl=1 1100w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/0-2.png?resize=272%2C182&amp;ssl=1 272w" width="1100"/>

  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   您还可以使用 Criptext 将安全电子邮件发送到非 Criptext 电子邮件地址。在这种情况下，信息使用 Signal 协议进行加密，密钥使用 AES 进行加密，并且收件人会在 Web 浏览器中收到显示加密消息的链接。一旦打开，在到期之前您将有10天的时间阅读电子邮件。
  </p>
  <p class="XzvDs _208Ie _2Dym_ blog-post-text-font blog-post-text-color _2p1aK _2R0Lu _2Dym_">
   至于桌面应用程序，它包括双因素身份验证，使用高级过滤器搜索，撰写窗口的 WYSIWYG 编辑器，全部/未读取的视图切换、标签和自动签名功能。它缺乏使用第三方电子邮件客户端或多帐户支持的能力。◾️
  </p>
