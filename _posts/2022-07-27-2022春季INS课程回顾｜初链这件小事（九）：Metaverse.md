  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   初链这件小事是2022年春季，中国美术学院网络社会研究所开设的新课程，本课程的主要目的是让非理工科背景的同学能够理解区块链与元宇宙基础知识，让大家能基于现有的开源工具与平台和基础的编程知识最大程度地参与到现有区块链与元宇宙项目中，思考新技术所带来的社会与伦理问题。
  </p>
  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   本课程主要分为三个部分：1. 基础背景知识回顾与掌握 2. 区块链的原理与生态 3. 元宇宙的愿景与现实。这个系列的文章由课堂录音整理而来，全系列共9讲。

  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   今天这节课给出的元宇宙的定义，来自不同的人或公司，希望能够让大家通过这些，来理解何谓“元宇宙”。《雪崩》中首次提到了“元宇宙”这个词，其实它就是一个由单个公司所控制的虚拟房地产世界。通过个人终端加上VR头显，就可以进入这样的世界。作为一名参与者，可以在其中购买和发展土地。

  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   维基百科里提到的“元宇宙”，在科幻小说中，大概意思就是利用AR，VR等设备进入到一个虚拟世界中。在这个世界里，不仅是3D的，你还可以和其他参与者来进行连接。过去“元宇宙”常常和先进的虚拟现实技术关联，其目的是增强沉浸感。而最近的“元宇宙”这个词会在web3里更多地出现。但无论如何，它还没有一个明确的定义。

   Roblox的这张图其实也给出了元宇宙的愿景，从这8个词里面已经可以看出很多端倪。比如说identity，抛开身份来谈数据和数据隐私是不实际的，身份在区块链世界里其实就是私钥。 我们希望所有制造出来的数据都是和我们的身份捆绑在一起的。但这里有一个问题，也是我们尝试去解决的一个问题，我们是没法成为自己资产的主人的，因为这些是被中心化平台所控制的，因此我们想找到一个方法去让我们成为自己资产的主人；friends就是可以和朋友在虚拟世界里一起互动；第三是immersive（沉浸式）；第四是low friction（低摩擦），拿着代币可以在元宇宙世界里无缝衔接，只要是同属于一个宇宙，都可以用同一种代币，这样可以提升效率；第五是variety,可以看到多元的东西。第六就是anywhere；第七是economy，经济系统；最后是civility。

  </p>

   <img alt="" class="wp-image-9270" data-attachment-id="9270" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-caption="" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3" data-large-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-1024x584.png" data-medium-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-300x171.png" data-orig-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3.png" data-orig-size="3184,1816" data-permalink="https://caa-ins.org/archives/9116/wechat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3" decoding="async" height="584" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" src="https://13.124.147.85/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-1024x584.png" srcset="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-1024x584.png 1024w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-300x171.png 300w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-768x438.png 768w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-1536x876.png 1536w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-2048x1168.png 2048w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-810x462.png 810w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat0fec8d963614c591b47ee6ce3e09cfe3-1140x650.png 1140w" width="1024"/>
  </figure>
  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   腾讯目前在做的超级QQ秀，可以看到已经做的非常精细。阿里巴巴最早是在2021年的演讲提到了元宇宙，但他们并没有特别强调元宇宙，只是想增强交互。
  </p>
  <figure class="wp-block-image size-large">
   <img alt="" class="wp-image-9271" data-attachment-id="9271" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-caption="" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c" data-large-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-1024x560.png" data-medium-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-300x164.png" data-orig-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c.png" data-orig-size="3252,1780" data-permalink="https://caa-ins.org/archives/9116/wechat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c" decoding="async" height="560" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" src="https://13.124.147.85/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-1024x560.png" srcset="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-1024x560.png 1024w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-300x164.png 300w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-768x420.png 768w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-1536x841.png 1536w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-2048x1121.png 2048w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-810x443.png 810w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChat41570110d0c5913bb64cb11dc6d99d3c-1140x624.png 1140w" width="1024"/>
  </figure>
  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   百度是第一个进军元宇宙的大型科技公司。推出的希壤可以让人们在穿戴设备后，参会、逛街、看展、交流。它不需要绑定唯一手机号或者唯一终端，这是一个非常大的突破。
  </p>
  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   现在存在的问题是，用户他们其实并不真正关心隐私的问题，因为巨头提供给他们的利益要更大。因此会有很多真正在意隐私，想改变现状的小项目会被排挤掉，会被这些大项目的糖衣炮弹，直接又简单的愿景所迷惑。
  </p>
  <figure class="wp-block-image size-large">
   <img alt="" class="wp-image-9272" data-attachment-id="9272" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-caption="" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="image-1" data-large-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-1024x595.png" data-medium-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-300x174.png" data-orig-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1.png" data-orig-size="1608,934" data-permalink="https://caa-ins.org/archives/9116/image-1-12" decoding="async" height="595" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" src="https://13.124.147.85/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-1024x595.png" srcset="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-1024x595.png 1024w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-300x174.png 300w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-768x446.png 768w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-1536x892.png 1536w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-810x470.png 810w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1-1140x662.png 1140w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/image-1.png 1608w" width="1024"/>
  </figure>
  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   元宇宙分为了7个layer，最里面是基础设施建设，比如5G，Cloud等。上面一层是人机交互，如智能穿戴设备，手机电脑等，可以看到这也是目前各大巨头在抢占的。第三层是去中心化。第四层是spatial computing，可以理解成为对场景的计算，各种建模。越往上就越贴近用户的体验。
  </p>
  <figure class="wp-block-image size-large">
   <img alt="" class="wp-image-9273" data-attachment-id="9273" data-comments-opened="0" data-image-caption="" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588" data-large-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-1024x575.png" data-medium-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-300x168.png" data-orig-file="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588.png" data-orig-size="3248,1824" data-permalink="https://caa-ins.org/archives/9116/wechate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588" decoding="async" height="575" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" src="https://13.124.147.85/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-1024x575.png" srcset="https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-1024x575.png 1024w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-300x168.png 300w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-768x431.png 768w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-1536x863.png 1536w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-2048x1150.png 2048w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-810x455.png 810w, https://caa-ins.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/07/WeChate583fc23178e2f159d12a7675723f588-1140x640.png 1140w" width="1024"/>

  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   元宇宙不一定要像头号玩家、黑客帝国和雪崩描述的那样，这只是元宇宙的一种形态，甚至是一个被过度宣传的buzzword（时髦术语），我们更希望大家能关注元宇宙的本质，关注元宇宙能够带来新的本该属于用户却不被用户拥有的权益。
  </p>
  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   最后作为这系列课程的总结，希望大家能去做一些NFT、social token、去中心化应用的实践，从用户的角度出发去思考如何build一个web3的世界，也希望大家更多地去关注网络研究所做的三个项目：Coop、archverse和Sparklink，或者也可以加入网络研究所同学发起的自治组织bAn。
  </p>
  <p class="has-normal-font-size">
   感谢大家的陪伴，希望我们之后还有机会开设其他课程。
  </p>
