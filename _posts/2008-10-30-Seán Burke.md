   <h1>
    Seán Burke
   </h1>
   <ul class="post-entry-meta">
    <li>
     By:
     <a href="http://www.ideobook.com/ming-shen/" title="查看 沈明 的作者主页">
      沈明
     </a>
     . 2008-10-30.
     <a href="http://www.ideobook.com/372/post-views-count/" title="统计说明">
      4,676
     </a>
    </li>
   </ul>
  </header>
  <div class="post-entry-content">
   <p>
    由于研究兴趣的关系，我曾读过一本书
    <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/redirect.html?ie=UTF8&amp;location=http://www.amazon.com/Death-Return-Author-Sean-Burke/dp/0748610065&amp;tag=oceintelleweb-20&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325">
     <em>
      The Death and Return of the Author: Criticism and Subjectivity in Barthes, Foucault and Derrida
     </em>
    </a>
    (Edinburgh University Press, 2nd edn, 1998)，作者是 Seán Burke。最近又找到一本主题相近的书
    <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/redirect.html?ie=UTF8&amp;location=http://www.amazon.com/Ethics-Writing-Authorship-Responsibility-Nietzsche/dp/0748618309&amp;tag=oceintelleweb-20&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325">
     <em>
      The Ethics of Writing: Authorship and Responsibility in Plato, Nietzsche, Levinas (and Derrida)
     </em>
    </a>
    (Edinburgh University Press, 2008)，准备读一下，发现作者也是 Seán Burke。于是不免有点好奇，想了解一下这个作者。在后面这本刚出版不久的新书封底上，对于作者是这样介绍的：
   </p>
   <blockquote>
    <p>
     Seán Burke was Lecturer, then Reader in English Studies at the University of Durham for thirteen years. His academic publications include
     <em>
      The Death and Return of the Author: Criticism and Subjectivity in Barthes, Foucault and Derrida
     </em>
     , and the critical edition,
     <em>
      Authorship: From Plato to the Postmodern
     </em>
     . His first novel, Deadwater (2002), has been published in France as
     <em>
      Au bout des docks
     </em>
     (2007).
    </p>
   </blockquote>
   <p>
    好一个“was”！现在呢？就 Google 一下他吧。结果令人意外的是，除去同名者之外，网上能找到的这个 Sean Burke 的资料很少。其中比较有用的是 elberry 写的一则短文 “
    <a href="http://elberry.wordpress.com/2007/04/16/sean-burke-irishman/">
     Sean Burke, Irishman
    </a>
    ”，读来令人感到几分伤感。
    <em>
     The Ethics of Writing
    </em>
    是今年才出版的，想来 Burke 应该还在人世吧。God bless him!
   </p>
