### 1: [2022-06-14 09:52:00+08:00 推文](https://twitter.com/SpeechFreedomCN/status/1536526838419410944)

南京女子丁燕，因于5月11日在微信群发表了一封写给习近平的公开信，促其放弃清零政策以保障民生，在当晚即被“查水表”。自由亚洲电台联系当地警方进行了确认，丁燕因此被关进了精神病院。丁燕此前曾多次因对社会事件发声而被抓，也曾多次被关进精神病院。<br><img style="" src="https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FVK69B1WIAAZOad?format=png&amp;name=orig" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">

### 2: [2022-06-14 09:58:00+08:00 推文](https://twitter.com/SpeechFreedomCN/status/1536528348419006464)

Nanjing woman Ding Yan, who posted her open letter to Xi Jinping in a WeChat group chat that requested him to abolish the Covid-zero Policy, arrested &amp; locked up in a mental asylum the same day. Ding had been arrested &amp; jailed in mental asylums several times for similar reasons.<br><img style="" src="https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FVK72w0XwAMaxXn?format=png&amp;name=orig" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">

### 3: [2022-06-16 11:22:00+08:00 推文](https://twitter.com/SpeechFreedomCN/status/1537274263446736896)
