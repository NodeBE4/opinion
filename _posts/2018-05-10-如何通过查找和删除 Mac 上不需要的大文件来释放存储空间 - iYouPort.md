   如何通过查找和删除 Mac 上不需要的大文件来释放存储空间
  </h1>

 <div class="entry-content">
  <p>
   释放整个硬盘驱动器上的磁盘空间可能很困难，尤其是当它充满了小文件时。但是，有一些优秀的 macOS 工具可以让你找到占用最多空间的文件并删除你不需要的文件。准备好清理磁盘了吗？
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    检查最可能藏污纳垢的地方
   </strong>
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8005 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 956px) 100vw, 956px" data-lazy-src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?resize=956%2C464&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?w=956&amp;ssl=1 956w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?resize=300%2C146&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?resize=768%2C373&amp;ssl=1 768w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="464" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?resize=956%2C464&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="956"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8005" data-recalc-dims="1" height="464" sizes="(max-width: 956px) 100vw, 956px" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?resize=956%2C464&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?w=956&amp;ssl=1 956w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?resize=300%2C146&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/9-7.png?resize=768%2C373&amp;ssl=1 768w" width="956"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p>
   您的大多数文件只会在几个文件夹中。下载就是其中之一，很多人填满了下载部分却忘了清空它，但是有些文件会隐藏在你想不到的地方。一个可以占用大量空间的地方是 iTunes 保存 iPhone 或 iPad 备份的文件夹。该文件夹位于：〜/ Library / Application Support / MobileSync / Backup
  </p>
  <p>
   根据设备的大小和备份频率，这些备份文件可能占用数十 GB。
  </p>
  <p>
   如果你有一段时间没有清空它，macOS 中的 Trash 文件夹也会占用相当多的空间，因此值得一看，看看你是否还在存储一些大文件。
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    手动查找大文件
   </strong>
  </p>
  <p>
   Finder 具有使用 Spotlight 搜索查找大型文件的内置方式。单击要搜索的驱动器，打开搜索窗口，然后单击右上角“保存”按钮旁边的 + 按钮。然后，您可以使用下拉菜单选择“文件大小”和“大于”选项。输入您要搜索的最小文件大小（超过 100MB 左右就可以了），您将看到超过该大小的所有文件的列表。然后，您可以选择要删除的项目。
  </p>
  <p>
   但是，此方法有两个缺点：
  </p>
  <ul>
   <li>
    Finder 仅显示超过特定大小的单个文件，而不是显示较小文件的文件夹，例如下载或程序安装；
   </li>
   <li>
    选择要删除的特定文件很困难，因为没有给出文件存储位置或它们可能有多重要的适当上下文；
   </li>
  </ul>
  <p>
   更好的界面是内置于 macOS 中的 Storage Management 应用程序，它显示按大小排序的所有文件，并显示最后访问它们的日期：
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8006 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 960px) 100vw, 960px" data-lazy-src="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?resize=960%2C626&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?w=960&amp;ssl=1 960w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?resize=300%2C196&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?resize=768%2C501&amp;ssl=1 768w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="626" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?resize=960%2C626&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="960"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8006" data-recalc-dims="1" height="626" sizes="(max-width: 960px) 100vw, 960px" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?resize=960%2C626&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?w=960&amp;ssl=1 960w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?resize=300%2C196&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/10-3.png?resize=768%2C501&amp;ssl=1 768w" width="960"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p>
   Storage Management 还有一些其他有用的工具。例如，您可以使用它来配置 iCloud 存储并将 Mac 设置为自动清空废纸篓。
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    使用第三方工具进行更好的控制
   </strong>
  </p>
  <p>
   磁盘清单 X 是一个出色的免费工具，用于可视化磁盘空间。您可以看到哪些部分占据空间最大。单击任意方块以查看关联的文件名。侧边栏上还有一个树状视图，可以让您查看每个文件夹占用的空间大小，您可以深入查找可能要删除的文件。可以右键单击每个文件以将其移至废纸篓或查看有关它的更多信息。
  </p>
  <p>
   但是，在使用磁盘清单 X 时，
   <strong>
    请记住一件重要的事。它确实显示系统文件，并允许您删除它们，因此除非您知道自己在做什么，否则您需要谨慎操作
   </strong>
   。
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8007 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 958px) 100vw, 958px" data-lazy-src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?resize=958%2C626&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?w=958&amp;ssl=1 958w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?resize=300%2C196&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?resize=768%2C502&amp;ssl=1 768w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="626" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?resize=958%2C626&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="958"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8007" data-recalc-dims="1" height="626" sizes="(max-width: 958px) 100vw, 958px" src="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?resize=958%2C626&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?w=958&amp;ssl=1 958w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?resize=300%2C196&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i0.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/1-24.png?resize=768%2C502&amp;ssl=1 768w" width="958"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p>
   自动清洁工具
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8008 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 962px) 100vw, 962px" data-lazy-src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?resize=962%2C600&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?w=962&amp;ssl=1 962w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?resize=300%2C187&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?resize=768%2C479&amp;ssl=1 768w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="600" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?resize=962%2C600&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="962"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8008" data-recalc-dims="1" height="600" sizes="(max-width: 962px) 100vw, 962px" src="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?resize=962%2C600&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?w=962&amp;ssl=1 962w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?resize=300%2C187&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i1.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/2-21.png?resize=768%2C479&amp;ssl=1 768w" width="962"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p>
   虽然这些手动工具对于查找大型文件很有用，但它们无法清理可能占用大量空间的系统垃圾和临时缓存，而且通常隐藏在您不想碰的系统文件夹中。这就是像
   <a href="http://macpaw.7eer.net/c/156932/63812/1733?subId1=content&amp;subId2=361957" rel="nofollow noopener" title="This link will take you away from steemit.com">
    Clean My Mac
   </a>
   这样的自动化工具派上用场的地方。当您启动应用程序时，它会对驱动器执行扫描，查看您可能在磁盘清单中遗漏的垃圾邮件和缓存文件夹。然后会自动清除它们。
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8009 jetpack-lazy-image" data-lazy-sizes="(max-width: 958px) 100vw, 958px" data-lazy-src="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?resize=958%2C602&amp;is-pending-load=1#038;ssl=1" data-lazy-srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?w=958&amp;ssl=1 958w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?resize=300%2C189&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?resize=768%2C483&amp;ssl=1 768w" data-recalc-dims="1" height="602" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?resize=958%2C602&amp;ssl=1" srcset="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="958"/>
   <noscript>
    <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8009" data-recalc-dims="1" height="602" sizes="(max-width: 958px) 100vw, 958px" src="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?resize=958%2C602&amp;ssl=1" srcset="https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?w=958&amp;ssl=1 958w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?resize=300%2C189&amp;ssl=1 300w, https://i2.wp.com/www.iyouport.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/3-19.png?resize=768%2C483&amp;ssl=1 768w" width="958"/>
   </noscript>
  </p>
  <p>
   Clean My Mac 还有一些其他有用的工具，例如卸载程序，用于清理旧应用程序，维护脚本和用于安全删除文件的碎纸机。它还有一个标签，可以让您查看大文件，虽然在列表视图中，而不是磁盘库存或 DaisyDisk 的分区界面。
  </p>
