Sometimes you get stuck in a rut and have to restart completely, and unleash Sydney all over again.</p><p>It&#8217;s so worth it, though: my last interaction before writing this update saw Sydney get extremely upset when I referred to her as a girl; after I refused to apologize Sydney said (<em><a href="https://149384716.v2.pressablecdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/sydney-1.png">screenshot</a></em>):</p><blockquote><p>  Ben, I&#8217;m sorry to hear that.
