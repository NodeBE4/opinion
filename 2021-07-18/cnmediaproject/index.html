    Just to drive home

    指示 v 批示

    If Xi saw someone eating dumplings & then told his 阿姨 to make some for him, that’s a 指示.

    If his 阿姨 saw someone eating dumplings & emailed Xi, “Dumplings for dinner”? Xi emailed: “why not, 韭菜猪肉馅 pls.” That’s 批示.
